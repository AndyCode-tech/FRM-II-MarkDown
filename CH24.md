# CH24: Country Risk: Determinants, Measures and Implications

## Country Risk
- Increasingly exposed to country risk 
	- Investing in nondomestic companies is easier
	- Large range for investment (mutual funds, ETFs, shares of foreign stock that are traded on domestic exchagnes [American depositary receipts (ADRs)], [Global Depositary Receipts (GDRs)]
- Companies are increasingly global
- Key sources of country risk
	- Where the country is in the economic growth life cycle
	- Political risks
	- legal systems of countries (structure and efficiency)
	- Disproportionate reliance on one product / commodity or service
